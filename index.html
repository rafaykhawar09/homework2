About

My name is Abdur Rafay Khawar and I was born and raised in Pakistan. I graduated from my university, National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, just this summer with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Sciences. Unforunately, I wasn't able to take up Web Development while I was there and so I started to learn it on my own.


I came to learn of this bootcamp program from my sister, whose been living in Seattle for the past couple of years, and after reading up on it, it just appealed to me because of it's more practical and hands-on approach.


I have always loved designing things, which is primarily one of the reasons that I love Web Development so much, especially the front-end part of it. My love for designing things, I suppose comes from my love from drawing - a hobby of mine. I love the artstyle of comicbooks, so I would make posters. My most beloved poster that I designed was a poster for Batman V Superman.
